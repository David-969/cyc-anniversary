Celebrating One Year of Achievements (2024 - 2025)

Introduction

On October 22, 2025, the Commonwealth Youth Council (CYC) marks one year since the current executives took office. This anniversary highlights the milestones achieved, challenges overcome, and the bright future ahead for youth empowerment worldwide.

Highlights & Achievements

Youth Empowerment Programs

Launched initiatives reaching over 50,000 young people globally.

Leadership Training

Organized 20+ leadership workshops across Commonwealth regions.

Policy Advocacy

Successfully influenced youth-friendly policies in multiple nations.

Global Partnerships

Strengthened collaborations with NGOs, governments, and youth bodies.

Accelerate Action - International Women's Day 2025

The Commonwealth Youth Council (CYC), working with the Programme for Africa Leadership at LSE (PFAL), is organising an event to commemorate International Women’s Day (IWD) on March 8, 2025, on the theme “For all women and girls: Rights, Equality and Empowerment #Accelerate Action.”.

The Apia Youth Declaration (2024)

Developed at the Commonwealth Youth Forum in October 2024 in Apia, Samoa, this declaration highlights young people’s key priorities and commitments across the Commonwealth. It addresses critical issues such as climate action, economic resilience, gender equality, and youth participation in governance. The document is a roadmap for collective action and collaboration, ensuring youth voices are at the forefront of Commonwealth developm.

Media & Engagement

Over the past year, CYC expanded its digital presence, connecting with millions of youth through social media campaigns, live webinars, and cross-cultural events.

Looking Ahead

Closing Note

This milestone is not just a celebration of the past year but a call to action for greater unity, inclusivity, and innovation in empowering young people everywhere.
